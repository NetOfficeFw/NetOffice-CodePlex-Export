Ok Ive figured it out:-
            Excel.OLEObject oleObjects = (Excel.OLEObject)xlWorkSheet.OLEObjects("CheckBox1");
            Excel.CheckBox CheckBox1 =  (Excel.CheckBox)oleObjects.Object;
            CheckBox1.Value = false;
This works fine now.
