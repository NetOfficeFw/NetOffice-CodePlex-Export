Question: What do I have to do to deliver my application with NetOffice?
Answer: Nothing except copying the needed assemblies to the target system. You don't need a registration or something like that with the exception of COMAddIns. Managed COMAddins generally have to be registered via the .Net Utility RegAsm.exe or the Windows Installer do that or you.


Question: Do COMAddins also work version independent?
Answer: Yes, of course. This is a particular strength of NetOffice. See the code examples for example project for all Office applications. 


Question: How do I migrate my Interop Assembly Code?
Answer: Change the references and the "using" statements. Occasionally, you need to make some adjustments to event triggers, Enums and constructor calls.
